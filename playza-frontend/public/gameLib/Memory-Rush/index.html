How to Play

  • A pattern of coloured tiles or symbols flashes on screen.
  • Memorize the sequence and order — both matter.
  • After it disappears, tap the tiles in the exact same order.
  • Wrong tap? You lose a life. Three lives per game.
  • Complete rounds to increase pattern length and earn more points.
  • Build a streak for a multiplier bonus!

Pattern types: 🎨 Colors · 🔷 Shapes · 🎭 Mixed
