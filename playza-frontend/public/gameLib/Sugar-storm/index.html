Sugar Storm Smash

Ride the candy tempest, trigger storm powers, and beat each sweet objective before the sugar squall burns out.

Guide

Match 3 to clear candies, match 4 or 5 to create power candies, chain special combos, and build Sugar Rush charge for storm clears.

Rules

Striped clears a line, wrapped blasts a zone, color bomb bends the board, and deeper levels add jelly, crates, sugar glass, and ingredient storms.

Progression

Early levels teach the basics, then the endless climb shifts to random board shapes, storm modes, layered blockers, and drop-objective levels.

Win Conditions

A level is complete only when you hit the target score and finish every active objective: candy orders, jelly, crates, sugar glass, and ingredient drops if they are present.

Swap neighboring candies to create matches of 3 or more.
