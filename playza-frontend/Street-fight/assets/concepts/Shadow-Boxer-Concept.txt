========================================
SHADOW BOXER - CHARACTER CONCEPT
========================================

CHARACTER NAME: Shadow Boxer
GAME: Street Fight

PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION:
- Gender: Male
- Age: 25-30
- Build: Athletic, muscular (boxer's physique)
- Height: 6'0" - 6'2" (183-188 cm)
- Weight: 200-220 lbs (91-100 kg)

CHARACTER TRAITS:
- Fighting Style: Street Boxing / Urban Combat
- Personality: Confident, mysterious, disciplined
- Signature Move: Shadow Step (quick dashes and combos)

OUTFIT DETAILS:

1. HOODIE:
   - Color: Matte black with subtle dark blue undertones
   - Material: Performance athletic fabric (moisture-wicking)
   - Style: Pullover hoodie, athletic cut
   - Neon accents:
     * Piping along hoodie seams in electric blue
     * Small "SF" logo on left chest in neon cyan
     * Hood drawstrings with neon tips
   - Hood: Up when not attacking, down during fights
   - Front: Zipperless, open at the collar showing bare chest/undershirt

2. FINGERLESS GLOVES:
   - Color: Black with neon blue trim
   - Material: Synthetic leather with gel padding
   - Length: Mid-forearm
   - Finger coverage: Fingers exposed except for thumb coverage
   - Padding: Reinforced knuckle areas
   - Wrist strap: Velcro closure with neon blue strip
   - Grip: Synthetic leather palm with grip texture

3. COMBAT SNEAKERS:
   - Color: Black with neon cyan accents
   - Style: High-top basketball/boxing hybrid
   - Sole: Thick, cushioned, slightly elevated heel
   - Ankle support: Padded high-top collar
   - Laces: Black with neon tips
   - Outsole: Rubber with herringbone traction pattern
   - Details: Small neon LED strips on sides (optional)

4. PANTS (if any):
   - Option A: Black athletic training pants
     * Material: Stretch polyester/spandex blend
     * Style: Tapered, above the ankle
     * Neon: Side stripes in electric blue
   - Option B: Shorts
     * Length: Mid-thigh
     * Material: Lightweight athletic fabric
     * Design: Elastic waistband with drawstring
     * Neon: Trim along hem

5. ADDITIONAL ACCESSORIES:
   - No shirt under hoodie (shows muscular torso)
   - Optional: Simple dog tag necklace
   - Optional: Wrist wraps under gloves

ANATOMY & PROPORTIONS:
- Head-to-body ratio: 7.5-8 heads tall
- Shoulder width: Broad, V-taper
- Chest: Defined pectorals, athletic
- Arms: Strong biceps/triceps, vascular
- Core: Six-pack definition visible
- Legs: Powerful quads and calves
- Posture: Balanced fighting stance (slight crouch)
- Expression: Confident smirk, focused eyes

POSE REQUIREMENTS:

FRONT VIEW:
- Standing fighting stance (left foot forward, right foot back)
- Fists up in guard position (right fist forward, left fist near face)
- Body slightly turned (3/4 view) or straight on
- Arms slightly bent, relaxed but ready
- Weight distributed evenly
- Head up, looking at opponent/opponent's level
- Show full body from head to feet

BACK VIEW:
- Same fighting stance
- Back turned to viewer
- Show hoodie from behind (hood down to see hair if any)
- Show glove backs, shoe soles, pant/shorts back
- Shoulder and back muscle definition visible
- Neck and head position same as front

SIDE VIEW:
- Profile view (left or right)
- Dynamic fighting stance
- Show depth of torso and limb proportions
- Highlight silhouette and movement lines

STYLE & RENDERING:
- Semi-realistic (not cartoon, not hyper-realistic)
- Video game concept art quality
- Clean linework with internal details
- Shaded to show form and volume
- High detail level (textures, wrinkles, seams)
- Similar to: Street Fighter 6, Tekken 8, Mortal Kombat 1 concept art

LIGHTING:
- Primary: Neon street lighting (blue/pink/purple hues)
- Secondary: Ambient city glow
- Rim lighting on edges
- Soft shadows under model
- Glow effects on neon accents
- Night atmosphere (dark background with neon highlights)

BACKGROUND:
- Transparent (PNG with alpha channel)
- OR minimal: Simple dark gradient
- No distracting elements
- Focus solely on character

OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS:
- Resolution: 4K (3840x2160) minimum
- Format: PNG with transparency
- Layers: Separate layers for character and lighting (optional)
- Turnaround: 1-2 weeks for both views
- File naming: Shadow-Boxer-Front-View.png, Shadow-Boxer-Back-View.png
- Include orthographic reference (optional)

3D MODELING NOTES:
- Clean topology for deformation
- Symmetrical where appropriate
- UV mapping considerations for specific areas:
  * Hoodie: Separate UV for hood, body, sleeves
  * Gloves: Separate for each hand
  * Shoes: Separate left/right
- Polygon budget: 15k-25k triangles for game model
- Texture size: 4K textures (albedo, normal, roughness, etc.)
- Joint locations must match concept proportions
- Pay attention to：
  * Shoulder rotation range (hoodie drape)
  * Elbow/knee bend (fabric wrinkles)
  * Finger articulation (glove fit)
  * Foot shape (shoe proportions)

COLOR PALETTE:
- Primary: #1a1a1a (black hoodie)
- Secondary: #0a0a14 (dark shadows)
- Accent 1: #00d4ff (electric blue neon)
- Accent 2: #ff00ff (magenta neon - optional)
- Skin: #c9a87c (athletic tan)
- Glove/Shoe: #1c1c1c (dark gray)

MOOD & ATMOSPHERE:
- Mysterious urban fighter
- Confident yet ready for battle
- Modern street culture aesthetic
- Cyberpunk/neon-noir influences
- Active, dynamic energy even in static pose

ARTIST NOTES:
- Reference boxer stance and posture
- Use anatomy references for muscular definition
- Pay attention to fabric drape and movement
- Neon elements should glow, not flat color
- Ensure silhouette reads well in profile
- Character should be recognizable from angle
- Consider weight shift in pose (more weight on back foot)
- Hands should look strong but not exaggerated

TECHNICAL SPECS:
- Canvas: 4000x4000 px minimum per view
- Lineart: Clean, black or dark lines
- Color: Rendered with shading/highlights
- Format: PNG with transparency
- DPI: 300 minimum for printing
